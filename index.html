Why Stories Matter

Sharing stories is not just about recalling the past; it is a vital tool for emotional well-being, cognitive health, and social connection.

  • 🧠 Cognitive Stimulation: Recalling memories helps keep the mind active and sharp.
  • 💖 Emotional Validation: Feeling heard and valued boosts self-esteem and reduces depression.
  • 🌉 Generational Bridge: Stories pass down invaluable wisdom and history to younger generations.
