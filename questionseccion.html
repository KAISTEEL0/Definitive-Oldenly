How to use

Find answers to your questions

How to upload a story?

  • 1 Click on the "Create Story" button located in the main menu
  • 2 Write your story content and add images or media files
  • 3 Review your story and tap "Publish" to make it live

How to make a donation?

  • 1 Navigate to the "Donate" section from the main navigation
  • 2 Select the amount you wish to donate and choose payment method
  • 3 Confirm your donation and complete the payment process

How to register?

  • 1 Go to the register button on the top right corner
  • 2 Answer the questions with your information
  • 3 Tap the register button to complete your registration
