Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At Oldenly, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Since Oldenly is a local demonstration platform, all your personal data, profile details, uploaded images, and activity logs are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (localStorage). We do not transmit, store, or process your data on any external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to personalize your experience on the platform, including:

  • Displaying your name, username, and avatar in the navbar and profile dashboard.
  • Managing your workshop registrations and available spots.
  • Displaying your recent activity timeline.

3. Data Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security of your browser and device. We recommend keeping your browser updated and securing your device with a password.

4. Deleting Your Data

You have complete control over your data. You can delete all your personal information and account details at any time by clicking the "Delete Account" button in the Settings tab of your Profile Dashboard, or by clearing your browser's cache and cookies.
