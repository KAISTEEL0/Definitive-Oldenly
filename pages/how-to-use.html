Quick help

Common questions and easy solutions

Use the search bar to quickly find the answer you need.

  • Check that your username and password are written correctly.
  • Make sure the Caps Lock key is not enabled.
  • If you forgot your password, use the reset option or contact support.
  • Verify that your speakers or headphones are connected.
  • Try refreshing the page and opening the story again.
  • If the problem continues, try another browser or device.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection before recording.
  • Save your progress before closing the page.
  • If the system stops, try again and submit your message later.
  • Use the form below to describe your issue briefly.
  • Include the device you are using and the browser.
  • Tell us what happened before the problem appeared.
